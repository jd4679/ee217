Episode Guide - episode 32

Episode title: Dante of the Deep Wood
Episode title (Japanese): 深い森のダンテ - Fukai Mori no Dante, "Dante of the Deep Wood"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 2
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': None

Synopsis

Wrath escapes from Ed and Al, with the help of some of the other Homonculus, while Gluttony and Lust incite a confrontation between the military and the Ishbal refugees. Ed and Al confront Izumi to find some hint of another way to restore their bodies, and she sends them to see a woman named Dante, who taught her alchemy. But who else awaits the Brothers Elric?
