Episode Guide - episode 4

Episode title: A Forger's Love
Episode title (Japanese): 愛の錬成 - Ai no rensei, "Forger's Love" (Note the word 'rensei', as in 'jintai rensei' meaning 'human transmutation')
Number of times Ed looks horrified: 1
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 1

Synopsis

Having burned down their house so that they can never return, Edward and Alphonse travel by train in the hopes that Ed can become a State Alchemist. On the way, they stop at a village where an old friend of their father lives - a village where it's rumoured that the dead come back to life.

Trivia

None.

Slip-ups

Skin-coloured gloves, perhaps?

If you look closely when Alphonse falls on top of Edward, you can see that they've mistakenly coloured Ed's gloves in flesh-tone instead of white. Even if he had removed his gloves, one of his hands would be mechanical, not flesh coloured.
