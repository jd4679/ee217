Episode Guide - episode 2

Episode title: Body of the Sanctioned
Episode title (Japanese): 禁忌の身体 - Kinki no karada, "Body of Taboo"
Number of times Ed looks horrified: 1
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 1

Synopsis

Edward and Alphonse, having stopped off at Lior City in search of the Philosopher's Stone, find themselves on the wrong side of the high priest Cornello after discovering that his 'miracles' are merely alchemy. The two brothers find themselves having to decide whether or not to let the city's population in on Cornello's secret.
