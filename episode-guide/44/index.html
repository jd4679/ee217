Episode Guide - episode 44

Episode title: Hoenheim of Light
Episode title (Japanese): 光のホーエンハイム - Hikari no Hoenheimu, "Hoenheim of Light"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 2
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 1

Synopsis

The true master of the Homonculus is revealed at long last! Ed and Al inform Mustang and company of the truth, then have a joyous reunion with their father. But where has the old man been, and what secrets does he know? Will Ed overcome his anger towards him?

Trivia

Hiromi Arakawa - more like Ara-COW-a?

Stephanie Kurniawan e-mails in this interesting snippet. "In episode 44, or around there, when Edward and Alphonse are confronted by Mustang, Edward tells him that the Fuhrer's secretary is a homunculus. Shieska thinks to herself "But she's an alien". When she does this, UFO's appear in the background, and one is beaming a cow. This cow is the representation Hiromi Arakawa uses to protray herself in her manga."

"This cow also has appear in another episode. I don't remember the episode number and furthur details. But it is when Ed is in the hospital and Winry comments on why he doesn't drink his milk."
