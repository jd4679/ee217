Episode Guide - episode 11

Episode title: The Other Brothers Elric
Episode title (Japanese): 砂礫の大地・前編 - Sareki no Daichi - Zenpen, "Pebbles of the Land - Part One"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 1
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': none - he managed to keep it bottled up

Synopsis

The brothers Elric arrive in the old mining town of Xenotime in search of the Philosophers' Stone - only to find that a pair of brothers have borrowed their names and now the townspeople think that the real brothers are impostors!

Trivia

mystik17_1998 writes in: "In an interview, the voice actor for Ed confessed at how he made Ed sound like he had a swollen jaw: sticking a powder doughnut in the side of his mouth. This wouldn't of been a problem, except at one time he had to keep a single doughnut in his mouth for an hour and the mush started to make him ill."
