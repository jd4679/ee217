Episode Guide - episode 35

Episode title: Reunion of the Fallen
Episode title (Japanese): 愚者の再会 - Gusha no Saikai, "Reunion of the Fallen"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 1
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 1

Synopsis

Ed, Al, and Winry head towards Ishbal, but encounter a girl on the road who tells them of her search for a man named Lujon, who is searching for a Philosopher's Stone to cure an epidemic in their village. Meanwhile, Lust is feeling strangely nostalgic, and a chance encounter with a man she knew years ago brings back memories of another epidemic, and a man she helped that time for reasons of her own...
