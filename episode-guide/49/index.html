Episode Guide - episode 49

Episode title: The Other Side of the Gate
Episode title (Japanese): 扉の向こうへ - Tobira no Mukou He, "To the Other Side of the Gate"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 4
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': None

Synopsis

Ed heads into the depths of the underground to face his destiny. The thing that was Archer and Izumi do battle. But what will happen when Ed faces Dante?

Trivia

Tobira no Mukou He

Tobira no Mukou is the name of one of the Fullmetal Alchemist ending themes.

Slip-ups

I got shot?

Nikki writes: "In the episode Izumi Curtis was shot in the right arm by Archer, which left a nasty scratch. But, when she was moved to the bench in the hall there was no marking to indicate that happened."
