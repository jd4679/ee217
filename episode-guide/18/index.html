Episode Guide - episode 18

Episode title: Marcoh's Notes
Episode title (Japanese): マルコー・ノート - Marukoo Nooto, "Marco Note"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 1
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 1

Synopsis

The brothers head to Central City to track down the notes Dr. Marcoh told Ed he'd hidden in the library, but it seems like they're not the only ones who have an interest in the notes--and the library has already been destroyed by the time Ed and Al arrive! A scatterbrained bookworm ex-librarian is their only hope!

Trivia

None.

Slip-ups

I guess he transmuted his belt

Mark L. Henry e-mails in to say this: "I found a slip-up while watching my DVD with a friend. When Ed and Al are talking to Marcoh about taking him to Risembool, his belt is missing. But when they go back to Ed his belt is back."
