Episode Guide - episode 39

Episode title: Secret of Ishbal
Episode title (Japanese): - 東方内戦 - Tohou Naisei, "Eastern Civil War"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 2
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 1

Synopsis

Winry and Scieszka head to Winry's home, hoping to meet Ed and Al there. Mustang and his subordinates head to Lior, where civil war is breaking out, and Mustang is less than pleased to learn of the presence of Kimbley among their number, but is unable to do anything about it. Ed learns the identity of their new travelling companion, and a confrontation seems inevitable, but Martel is interested in vengeance on the ones who betrayed her and the homonculus. The brothers learn from her what happened at Ishbal years before, during the war. But when they are found by the military, they learn that Lior is engaging in resistance to the military, and are obligated to join in and participate in the suppression...
