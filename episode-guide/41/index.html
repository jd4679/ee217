Episode Guide - episode 41

Episode title: Holy Mother
Episode title (Japanese): 聖母 - Seiba, "Holy Mother"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 2
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': None

Synopsis

Scar stirs rebellion to further his personal vendetta, while Ed is held to prevent his interference, though he does send a message to Mustang from within the city, warning them of Scar's intentions. Kimbley and the chimeras created by Tucker run rampant inside Lior. Al, having learned the truth about the Fuhrer, worries about his brother, and heads into the city to find him, but will he arrive in time to prevent Ed from coming to harm?
