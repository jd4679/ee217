Episode Guide - episode 26

Episode title: Her Reason
Episode title (Japanese): 彼女の理由 - Kanojo no Riyuu, "Her Reason"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: None
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 1

Synopsis

Ed, Al, and Winry stop in Rush Valley, to check out the automail shops there to satisfy Winry's curiosity. Ed uses alchemy to stop a rigged-armwrestling game, upsetting Winry by not having confidence in her work. A chance encounter gives Winry an opportunity for revenge, and a chance to help a new friend!

Trivia
