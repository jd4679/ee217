Episode Guide - episode 13

Episode title: Flame vs Fullmetal
Episode title (Japanese): 焔 vs 鋼 - Honoo vs Hagane, "Flame vs Steel"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: none
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 2

Synopsis

Ed and Al return to the military at last, and Ed gives his report of their travels. Ed is convinced that Colonel Mustang knows more than he's telling about the mysterious Dr. Marcoh and seizes on a perfect opportunity to make Mustang talk! Also, puppies and kittens.
