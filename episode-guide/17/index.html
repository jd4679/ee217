Episode Guide - episode 17

Episode title: House of the Waiting Family
Episode title (Japanese): 家族の待つ家 - Kazoku no Motsu Ie, "House of the Waiting Family"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 0
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': 3
Number of times Armstrong loses his shirt: 3

Synopsis

Ed and Al make it back home for repairs, but Winry isn't too pleased at the damage to her work. In order to allow time for maintenance and repairs, they have to take a three-day vacation. While they're there, Ed and Al visit more than just Winry and her grandma...

Trivia

Slip-ups

Animegirl writes: " I've noticed a lot lately that Edward, although genuinely mature, acts completely childish every now and then. If you exclude the constant "flipping out when being called short" incidents, Ed makes several comments such as "I don't like shots", "I don't want to be board", and, of course, "I hate milk!" I don't remember exactly where I heard it, but I'm pretty sure that Ed likes flavored milk, like strawberry and coffee flavored, and I know for sure that Ed likes the milk in stew, saying so in one of his flashbacks."
