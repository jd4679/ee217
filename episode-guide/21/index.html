Episode Guide - episode 21

Episode title: The Red Glow
Episode title (Japanese): 紅い輝き - Akai Kogayaki, "Crimson Glow"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 4
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': None.

Synopsis

Al finds himself suffering from doubts about his existence, slowing his combat instincts and putting him in danger. Meanwhile, Ed's opponents, now defeated, offer Ed a dilemma which hits a little too close to home. Scar's arrival on the scene and the laboratory's other inhabitants breaking loose complicate matters even more...and something that was sealed inside worries even Lust! What other terrible discoveries await them in the depths of the lab?!
