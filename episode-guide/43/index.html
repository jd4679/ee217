Episode Guide - episode 43

Episode title: The Stray Dog
Episode title (Japanese): 野良犬は逃げ出した - Norai Inu wa Nigedashita, "The Stray Dog Has Fled"
Number of times Ed looks shocked: 1
Number of times Ed flips out because someone calls him 'short': None

Synopsis

Winry and Scieszka return to Winry's home, but a strange older man seems to be following them! Who is he, and how does he know Winry's family? Ed and Al, in the meantime, are on the move, not wishing to fall into the hands of the Homonculus. Ed decides to head home as well, planning to retrieve something to help him fight against Sloth... Mustang and the others investigate what occurred at Lior, trying to piece together what happened, but the Fuhrer seems to suspect that the Elric brothers were in league with Scar or the Homonculus, and responsible for the destruction!
